  🔍 How Cost Measurement Works

  IG.com (Automated):
  - Places order at 20:50 UK
  - Records: bid, ask, mid, fill price
  - Calculates spread: (fill - mid) / mid
  - Places second order at 21:00 UK
  - Compares costs
  - Measures timing penalty directly

  IBKR (Semi-Automated):
  - Shows instructions
  - You place order, enter fill data
  - Script records same metrics

  Calibration (After 10 trades):
  Total Costs = Avg Spread + Avg Slippage + Timing Penalty

  If < 5%:  Strategy EXCELLENT (25-35% CAGR)
  If 5-8%:  Strategy GOOD (15-25% CAGR)
  If 8-10%: Strategy MARGINAL (10-15% CAGR)
  If > 10%: Strategy NOT VIABLE (don't trade)

  No more guessing. Your data decides.
